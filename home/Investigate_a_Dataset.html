Introduction¶

Dataset Description¶

This dataset collects information from 100k medical appointments in Brazil and is focused on the question of whether or not patients show up for their appointment. A number of characteristics about the patient are included in each row.

columns Description in dataset :¶

  • PatientId - Identification of Patient.
  • AppointmentID - Identification of Appointment.
  • Gender -Male or Female (M,F).
  • ScheduledDay - The day that patient registered an appointment.
  • AppointmentDay - The day of appointment.
  • Age - Age of patient.
  • Neighbourhood - The place where the appointment takes place.
  • Scholarship - In Brazil,Scholarship as a subsidy for very low-income families if they have they children going to school and other more specific requirements(0 who have't Scholarship ,1 who have Scholarship).
  • Hipertension - People who have hypertension (0 not have ,1 have ).
  • Diabetes - People who have Diabetes or not (0 not have ,1 have ).
  • Alcoholism - People who have Alcoholism or not (0 not have ,1 have ).
  • Handcap - People who have Handcap (0,1,2,3,4).
  • SMS_received - messages sent to the patient or not (0,1)
  • No-show - patient show up or not ('Yes' no show ,'No' show up)

def Age_noshow_Plot(df, grpVar, yVar=None, color='blue',title=None, ylabel=None, rotation=None, figsize=(14,6), alpha=0.7):
    '''base function for groupby plotting of dependent variables to no_show column'''
    # plotting
    df.groupby([grpVar])[yVar].mean().plot.bar(edgecolor='black', figsize=figsize, rot=0, width=0.6, color=color, alpha=alpha)
    plt.title(f' {title}'.title(), fontsize = 14, weight = "bold")
    plt.xlabel(grpVar.title(), fontsize =10, weight = "bold")
    plt.ylabel(f'{ylabel}'.title(), fontsize = 10, weight='bold')
    plt.xticks(rotation=rotation)
    plt.grid(axis='y')
